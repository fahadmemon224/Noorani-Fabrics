Let About NOORANI Fabrics

Innovative Design
for PAKISTANI fashion

Our journey started in 1986 when NOORANI FABRICS was setup to provide innovative textile solutions across the globe. Soon, NOORANI FABRICS emerged as one of the leading textile manufacturing and exporting companies of Pakistan. For more than 35 years, NOORANI’s reputation and distinctive image have been consistently developed across an expanding number of products, brands, and international markets. Currently, NOORANI FABRICS is the largest vertically integrated textile setup in Pakistan.
